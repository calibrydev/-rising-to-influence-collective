Who We Are

Rising to Influence is dedicated to empowering young individuals through education, mentorship, and valuable knowledge

Our Purpose

To equip and inspire young people with the knowledge, skills, and mentorship they need to become positive influencers, driving meaningful change in their communities and fields of influence.

Our Vision

To see a generation empowered with responsibility and purpose rising as positive influencers.

Our Mission

We cultivate leadership, personal growth, and a strong sense of purpose, enabling individuals to rise as impactful leaders in their respective domains.

Terms & Conditions

Membership guidelines and organizational policies

Membership Terms & Conditions

  1. Membership categories are distinct with defined rights and responsibilities.
  2. Registration fee for Registered membership is non-refundable; Premium is renewable annually.
  3. All members and associates must uphold RTIC's values: clarity, stewardship, and professional excellence.
  4. Members and associates must maintain confidentiality of RTIC's intellectual property and communication standards.
  5. Certificates are issued only to Registered members upon completion of training.
  6. Volunteers do not hold membership rights unless registered.
  7. Partners are recognized but have no voting rights or certificates.
  8. RTIC may revoke membership or affiliation for misconduct or breach of confidentiality.
  9. Personal and organizational data will be used in compliance with applicable laws.
  10. Members must adhere to organizational policies and participate in events according to guidelines.

Values & Conduct

  • RTIC encourages Christian values while welcoming diverse beliefs in a respectful, inclusive manner
  • Members are expected to adhere to all organizational policies at departmental and program levels

Exiting Process

  • Members wishing to leave must notify the secretary
  • Surveys may be conducted to record reasons for leaving
  • Prolonged inactivity may be considered as voluntary exit

Member Participation & Access

Membership in RTIC provides access, not obligation.

  • Members may participate in programs offered under any pillar or department according to their interest, readiness, and season of life.
  • Participation is voluntary, not compulsory.
  • Faith-based programs (such as discipleship) are optional, while neutral programs (such as book clubs, leadership development, or career sessions) are open to all members who align with RTIC's values and conduct.

RTIC respects that growth is personal, paced, and purposeful, not uniform.

Volunteering & Contribution

Beyond program participation, members are encouraged to contribute through service.

  • Members may volunteer within programs, departments, or community initiatives.
  • Volunteers serve under the guidance of leaders and are supported through structured coordination and care.
  • Volunteering is viewed as a pathway for growth, responsibility, and leadership development, not just task completion.

Volunteers are embedded across RTIC and coordinated through the Secretariat, ensuring dignity, accountability, and support.
